About me

I received the B.E. degree from the Northeastern University University (China) of Automation, in 2018, the M.S. degree from University of Illinois at Chicago major in ECE, in 2020, and the M.S. degree from Boston University major in ME, in 2022. I am currently an Algorithm Engineer with the Vicente Medical Robot. My main research interests include Robot Control, Optimizaiton Algorithm, and Computer Vision. Also, I do some works about MBSE and CPS. I focus on using math and automated tools to solve practical problems.
